Memento

Release date: March 16, 2001 (USA)
Director: Christopher Nolan
Screenplay: Christopher Nolan
Adapted from: Memento Mori
Cinematography: Wally Pfister
Budget: 9 million USD

Description

Leonard Shelby, an insurance investigator,
suffers from anterograde amnesia and uses notes and tattoos to hunt for the man he thinks killed his wife, which is the last thing he remembers.
